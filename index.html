Parameter space of the Henon map

Based on Structure of the parameter space of the Henon map by Jason A.C. Gallas

This diagram shows how the periodicity of orbits changes for different values of the parameters a and b. The first 64 iterations of the point (1,0) are calculated and the period of the resulting orbit is identified. Black regions show infinite divergence, and colours are assigned to regions of finite periodicity.

Click inside the rectangle to see the period and a/b values at that point. Click on a point while holding <Shift>(<Alt>) to zoom in (out) by a factor of 2 at that point.
